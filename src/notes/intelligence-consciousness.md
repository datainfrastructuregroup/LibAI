## Defining Intelligence

Traditional views of intelligence often focus on:
- Problem-solving capabilities
- Learning and adaptation
- Abstract reasoning
- Pattern recognition

However, AI systems challenge these definitions by demonstrating capabilities that appear intelligent without necessarily possessing understanding or consciousness.

## AI and Human Intelligence

The relationship between artificial and human intelligence raises critical questions:

### Computational Intelligence
- Pattern matching at scale
- Statistical inference
- Optimization algorithms
- Data processing capabilities

### Human Intelligence
- Embodied cognition
- Emotional understanding
- Social cognition
- Creative synthesis

## Consciousness Considerations

The gap between intelligence and consciousness remains profound. AI systems can:
- Process information
- Generate responses
- Solve complex problems
- Learn from experience

But they may lack:
- Subjective experience
- Self-awareness
- Qualitative consciousness
- Intentionality
