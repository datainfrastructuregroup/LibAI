# Productivity Systems

Productivity is about managing your energy and attention, not just your time. Here are some key approaches:

## Getting Things Done (GTD)

[[GTD]] is a methodology developed by David Allen. The core principles:
- Capture everything
- Clarify what it means
- Organize into actionable items
- Reflect regularly
- Engage with confidence

## Time Blocking

[[Time-blocking]] involves scheduling specific blocks of time for different activities. This helps with:
- Deep work sessions
- Context switching reduction
- Better work-life balance

## The Pomodoro Technique

[[Pomodoro-technique]] uses 25-minute focused work sessions with 5-minute breaks. Benefits:
- Prevents burnout
- Maintains focus
- Breaks large tasks into manageable chunks
